Dark Patterns

Dark pattern design choices exploit user psychology to the benefit of the developer or company. These manipulative tricks coerce or decieve users to take actions they otherwise would not have. Deceptive design and dark patterns frequently find themselves in the digital marketplace, where users can be pushed to purchase a product, service, or subscription.
